Pricing

Two options, depending on where you're applying

Both options include the same two introductory sessions and fifteen individual coaching sessions. The difference is how many schools' essays we cover along the way.

UC Applicants Only

$800

2 introductory sessions + 15 individual sessions

  • Covers all four required UC Personal Insight Questions
  • One-on-one, 30-minute weekly sessions
  • Editing and revision support throughout

UC + Other Schools

$1200

2 introductory sessions + 15 individual sessions

  • Everything in the UC-only plan
  • Covers additional essays for private and other public schools
  • Best for students applying broadly

A few things to know

  • Students must be UC-eligible to be accepted into the Academy
  • Payment is accepted by check or Zelle
  • Weekly session times are arranged around your schedule
  • Sessions run 30 minutes each, once a week
