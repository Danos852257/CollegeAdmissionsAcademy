Welcome to The College Admissions Academy!

An individualized program offering essay-writing support to students applying to the University of California and beyond.

Who this program is for:

Rising Seniors

Start early and walk into fall application season with a plan and drafts already underway.

Transfer Students

Community college transfer applicants get the same close, structured support for their UC essays.

Beyond the UCs

Applying to private or other public schools too? We scale to cover those essays as well.

What students gain from the process
  • Turning scattered ideas into clear, well-formed essays
  • Expert, line-by-line editing and revision
  • Clear guidelines for grammar, punctuation, and formal writing
  • Help narrowing down the right essay topics
  • Vocabulary that reads as college-level
  • Accountability for deadlines and progress
  • An objective perspective
  • Sessions students look forward to
97% of Academy students were accepted to at least one UC they applied to.
